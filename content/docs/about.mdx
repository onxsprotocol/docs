## About Oorth Nexus

Oorth Nexus Protocol is a Blockchain-as-a-Service platform for ethical and halal industries. It focuses on transparency, compliance, and scalability for enterprise use cases.

## Company Background

Infrastructure since 2010.

PT. Xyz Inovasi Digital was founded in 2010 as a technology company focused on research and development. With headquarters in Jakarta, Indonesia, the team has spent over a decade building solutions for enterprise challenges.

## Why the Halal Industry Needs Blockchain

- Fraud and mislabeling can undermine consumer trust and violate religious standards.
- Complex supply chains make it difficult to verify the origin and handling of halal products.
- Manual certification processes are slow, costly, and prone to errors.
- Regulatory requirements differ across jurisdictions and are difficult to reconcile.

## How Oorth Nexus Solves These Challenges

- Immutable record-keeping prevents falsification of halal certificates.
- Real-time traceability from farm to consumer table.
- Automated smart contracts for instant certification verification.
- Unified compliance framework across multiple jurisdictions.
- Decentralized storage ensures data integrity and accessibility.
- Transparent audit trails for regulatory reporting.

## Principles

- Enterprise ready for halal supply chains, Islamic finance, and sustainable business.
- Private by design with smart contract programmability to meet confidentiality needs.
- Share only what is necessary for audits or regulation while protecting data.
- Fully programmable digital assets to build privacy-first applications at scale.
