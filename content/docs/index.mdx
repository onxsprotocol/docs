Oorth Nexus Protocol delivers a proprietary Layer 1 blockchain enhanced with Layer 2 rollup technology. EVM-compatible, scalable, and built for transparency across halal supply chains, Islamic finance, and sustainable business practices.

## What You Can Build

- Enterprise-grade blockchain infrastructure with compliance in mind.
- Transparent, immutable records for halal certification and supply chain traceability.
- Privacy-first applications powered by programmable smart contracts.

## Core Stack

- Layer 1 blockchain with Layer 2 rollup scalability.
- Multi-layer infrastructure from edge to fog to blockchain.
- Sovereign Data Objects (SDO) to keep ownership with the data owner.
