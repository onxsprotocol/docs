## Built for Real-World Impact

Oorth Nexus supports a broad set of enterprise and public-good use cases with transparent, ethical blockchain infrastructure.

## Primary Use Cases

- Halal Supply Chain: track and verify halal certification from source to consumer with immutable records.
- Islamic Finance: shariah-compliant financial products and transparent transaction records.
- Sustainability: carbon tracking and ESG reporting for environmentally conscious businesses.
- Charitable Giving: transparent zakat and sadaqah distribution with full audit trails.
- Enterprise Solutions: private permissioned networks for enterprise blockchain applications.
- Global Trade: cross-border payments and trade finance with reduced friction.

## Native Applications

- Halal Trace: supply chain verification for halal products.
- Sukuk Exchange: Islamic finance instruments on-chain.
- Carbon Credits: transparent sustainability marketplaces.
- Zakat Portal: accountable distribution and reporting.
